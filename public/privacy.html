Privacy Policy for ReadyReply AI

Last Updated: March 2026

ReadyReply AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data. Our extension is designed with a strict "privacy-first" architecture.

1. Information We Access

ReadyReply AI operates exclusively within the `mail.google.com` environment. The extension only accesses the text of the specific email thread you are currently viewing and only does so when you explicitly click the "ReadyReply" button.

2. How Your Data is Used

The sole purpose of accessing your email text is to generate a relevant, professional draft response. The email text is sent directly to Google's Gemini API using the API key that you provide and store locally on your own device. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your data. ReadyReply AI does not have external databases or servers. Your Gemini API key is stored locally in your browser's secure storage. The email content processed by the extension is never saved, logged, or retained by us in any form.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to any third parties. Your data is only transmitted to the Google Gemini API for the strict purpose of generating the requested email reply.
